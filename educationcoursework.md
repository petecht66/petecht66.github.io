### Bachelor's Degree
I earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Statistics (with minors in Computer Science and Economics) from the University of Delaware in December of 2025. I achieved a cumulative GPA of 3.94, cum laude honors at graduation, Dean's List honors for all seven semesters, and the General Honors Award from the University of Delaware Honors College. My coursework had exceptional variety, including courses in statistics and mathematics, computer science, economics, and sports analytics. This page highlights relevant courses that I completed during my undergraduate career at the University of Delaware.
