### Sports Science and Analytics Intern with the University of Delaware Athletic Department

From August 26th of 2024 to December 15th of 2025, I worked as a Sports Science and Analytics Intern with the University of Delaware Athletic Department. This internship provided me with sports analytics experience with several Varsity athletics teams at the University of Delaware Athletic Department, including football, men's and women's soccer, men's and women's basketball, and men's and women's lacrosse. My primary responsibility involved the administration of STATSports GPS trackers for our outdoor teams, Firstbeat heart rate monitors for our basketball teams, and force plate testing using Hawkin Dynamics. Through communication with strength and conditioning coaches and athletic trainers, this data was used to mitigate the risk of injury for our Blue Hen student-athletes. During basketball and lacrosse games, I tracked shooting data (location, make/miss, save/goal, etc.) using ShotPlotter. For our soccer teams and coaching staffs, I live tagged game events (dangerous services, set pieces, attacking 3rd entries, etc.) using Spiideo.
